Effective Date: 5 June 2023

This Privacy Policy governs the manner in which “Pulse: Discover movies and TV Shows” ("we" or "our") collects, uses, maintains, and discloses information collected from users ("users" or "you") of the Pulse mobile application ("app") on Android and iOS platforms.

Information We Collect: We may collect the following types of information from users:

  • Information: We may ask for permissions to access your device's notifications, camera, gallery, and other device features. These permissions are explicitly requested and used only for necessary purposes, such as sending notifications, adding display pictures, and inviting other users to the app. We do not collect any personal information without your explicit consent.
  • Usage Data: We collect transactional data generated through your usage of the app, such as likes, dislikes, watchlist activities, and recommendations shared with other users. This data is used to enhance your app experience and provide personalized content.

    • Third-Party Services: We integrate the following third-party services into our app:

  • Firebase: We use Firebase for app analytics, crash reporting, and authentication. Firebase may collect user data as described in their privacy policy. Please review Firebase's privacy policy for more information.


    • Use of Collected Information: We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Personalised Content: The collected data is used to provide personalised content and recommendations based on your preferences.
  • App Functionality: We use the information to improve the functionality of the app, enhance performance, and troubleshoot issues.
  • Communication: If you choose to invite other users, we may use your contacts to facilitate the invitation process. We do not share your contacts' information with any third parties.


    • Data Storage and Security:

  • We store the collected data in secure servers provided by Google Cloud Storage/Firebase. We do not store any user data locally on the device.


    • Data Sharing:

  • We do not share user data with any third-party services, partners, or advertisers. Your information is kept confidential and is only used for the purposes mentioned in this Privacy Policy.


    • User Rights and Control: You have the following rights and control over your personal information:

  • Access, Modification, and Deletion: You can request access to your personal information, modify any inaccuracies, or delete your data by emailing us at admin@vybetech.in We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe.
  • Opt-Out of Data Collection: As of now, users cannot opt-out of data collection. However, we are working on implementing this feature in the future, allowing users to manage their data preferences within the app.


    • Updates to the Privacy Policy

  • We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting the revised Privacy Policy on our app. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates. We may also notify you of any significant changes via email or through app notifications.
